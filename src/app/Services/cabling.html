Network Cabling

Tech On-Site is your trusted source for all your business or residential data cabling needs. Whether you need a line run from the basement to another floor, from one room or office to another or a patch panel installed with multiple cables run to distant offices, Tech On-Site techs can take care of it, cleanly and professionally.

One application we frequently encounter is when a customer's wireless router can't reach an area where they need wireless access. To solve this problem, we typically run an ethernet cable from the router to the area in need of service and install a wireless access point. Wireless devices can then get full-strength WiFi signals which optimizes data speeds.
