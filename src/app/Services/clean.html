Malware Removal & Security Solutions

Viruses, Trojan horses, rootkits, worms, spyware and adware - collectively called “malware” (short for “malicious software”) - are common and significant issues for PC users. The problems they cause include annoying pop-ups, significant performance degradation, data loss, system instability and even complete system failure. Some malware present serious threats to financial or other sensitive data and personal privacy. Any malware issue should be considered potentially dangerous and eliminated immediately.

Many of our customers ask, “How could I possibly have a virus? I installed antivirus software.” The answer is that malware is software code written by malicious programmers, many of whom are very good at what they do. They find ways to circumvent antivirus protection to implement their attacks. Although most antivirus applications are quite effective, none are 100% capable of preventing all malware infections. 

Tech On-Site has the tools and expertise to eliminate malware and optimally secure your PC to best prevent future infections. We’ll also educate users how to safely interact with the Internet and avoid further malware problems.
